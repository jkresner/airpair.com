AirPair.com Site Updates

We hope you've enjoyed seeing all the new designs and features appearing each week on AirPair.com. We known things haven't felt cohesive. The good news is we're now half way through the v1 road map and are starting to reconnect the dots to make a more awesome experience than ever before. Thanks for being patient, we have loved all your feedback and bug reports so far. You guys are great!

Recenly added in the last 8 weeks

  • Improved our post authoring tools on a new github backed flow for far better collaboration.
  • We've be finalized the get help form.
  • Our new administrative CRM is complete, meaning faster customer service, and turn around time for pairing with an expert. We're excited to focus our energy on customer and expert facing parts of the site from now through to March!
  • Integrated site chat is now in beta! You can now speak to experts and agree on mutually acceptable times before you pay. No more HipChat (thank god!).
  • Migration of Expert Payouts for existing 2014 experts from the old site is complete.

Coming in the next 4 weeks

  • Drastically improving the expert reply process (a very rough basic version was in place)
  • New expert profiles and settings are on their way (in case you've been wondering how to become an expert).
  • Tieing the whole AirPair experience together with a Dashboard showing upcoming AirPairs & personalized content based on your stack and bookmarks.
  • Quite a few cools things we don't want to publicily annouce yet :)
